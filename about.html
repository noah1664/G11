FAQ

  • How long does shipping usually take?
  • If you are living in Canada shipping should take about 5 business days. International shipping usually takes 2 weeks to a month
  • How much does shipping cost?
  • Shipping within Canada is free. Shipping internationally costs $15. If your order is over $100 we will provide free international shipping
  • How do I cancel my order?
  • Orders can be cancelled in the my orders section of your account information. If the order has already been shipped we cannot cancel your order.
  • What methods of payment do you accept.
  • We accept paypal, VISA and Mastercard.
  • How long does it take for my order to be shipped?
  • Orders usually leave our office 2-4 days after the order has been placed depending on how busy we are.
